Description

Gung Cho' is a 2D platformer where Cho', the protagonist, must reach the summit of Mount Xyaha to recover the crystals that hold the lost culture of his ancestors. It was developed in Unity by a team of six people.

For more information about the game, you can visit the website and watch the final trailer.
